# ScamShield Nigeria

Nigeria loses over ₦5 billion annually to cybercrime. Scammers specifically 
target Nigerians through WhatsApp — exploiting trust, urgency, and low 
digital literacy. Fake bank alerts have destroyed small businesses. OTP 
theft has wiped out savings overnight. Yet most Nigerians have no tool to 
verify suspicious messages before it is too late. ScamShield was built to 
give every Nigerian — regardless of education level or location — a free, 
instant, AI-powered shield against fraud in their own language.

ScamShield Nigeria is an AI-powered cybersecurity platform built to protect 
Nigerians from online scams and fraud. It features a WhatsApp message scanner, fake bank alert detector using Claude Vision AI, phishing link checker, community scam reporting, phone number lookup, and an interactive scam intelligence map showing fraud hotspots across all 36 states. SABI, our Nigeria-trained AI chatbot, provides real-time cybersecurity guidance in English and Pidgin. The platform helps everyday Nigerians — including those with low digital literacy — identify and avoid scams before losing money.
