🚩 Signs of a Fake Alert
  • 🔴 Sender name doesn't match bank
  • 🔴 Wrong logo, font, or colors
  • 🔴 Misspelled words ("Creditted")
  • 🔴 Missing transaction reference
  • 🔴 Unusual amount formatting
  • 🔴 Screenshot looks edited/cut
  • 🔴 Pressure language ("Act NOW")
