📋 What Makes a Good Report
  • ✔️ The scammer's phone number
  • ✔️ Exactly what they said / promised
  • ✔️ How they contacted you (WhatsApp, SMS, call)
  • ✔️ Screenshot with your personal details blurred
  • ✔️ The state / location where it happened
⚠️ Common Warning Signs
  • 🚩 "Send small fee to receive big prize"
  • 🚩 Unsolicited "credit alert" screenshots
  • 🚩 Urgency: "act within 1 hour or lose access"
  • 🚩 Requests for OTP, PIN, or BVN
  • 🚩 Promises of 100%+ investment returns
