💡 Nigeria Cybersecurity Tips
🏦
Your Bank Will NEVER
ask for your PIN, OTP, or BVN via WhatsApp, SMS, or phone call. If anyone does this — it's a scam. Hang up and call your bank's official number.
💸
Verify Before Releasing Goods
Always confirm credit alerts in your bank app — not from screenshots. Fake alerts are the #1 tool used to scam market traders and business owners in Nigeria.
🔗
Never Click Unverified Links
Shortened links (bit.ly, tinyurl) in WhatsApp messages almost always lead to phishing sites. Paste the message into ScamShield first.
